# 🎮 Learning Game Prototypes

These are playable prototypes created recently that demonstrate my instructional game design work.

## Play the Games (they're all 5 minutes or less)

- 🧩 **Small Choice, Mighty Judgment!**  
  ▶ https://lesleylearnification.github.io/small-choice-mighty-judgment/

  Problem: Employees know the handbook but freeze when real customers don't follow the script.
  Solution: Transform knowledge of handbook stuff into confident frontline decisions.


- 🧩 **Small Question, Mighty Sale!**  
  ▶ https://lesleylearnification.github.io/small-question-mighty-sale/

  Problem: Employees know the products, but recommend the wrong solution.
  Solution: Improve consultative selling and increase confidence in product recommendations.


🧩 **Small Steps, Mighty Standards!**  
  ▶ https://lesleylearnification.github.io/small-steps-mighty-standards/

  Problem: Employees remember procedures until something unexpected happens.
  Solution: Build procedural fluency instead of rote memorization to reduce costly errors.


🧩 **Small Update, Mighty Agility!**  
  ▶ https://lesleylearnification.github.io/small-update-mighty-agility/

  Problem: Business changes faster than the training.
  Solution: Keep distributed teams current without lengthy retraining whenever business evolves
